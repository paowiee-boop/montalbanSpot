MT. BALAGBAG

Mount Balagbag is a mountain situated in the Sierra Madre range, primarily located in the municipality of Rodriguez, Rizal, Philippines. It's known for its relatively easy hiking trails, offering scenic views of the surrounding area including parts of Metro Manila and the Ipo Watershed, making it a popular choice for day hikes from the city.

NAME ORIGIN

"Balagbag" comes from the Tagalog word "pabalagbag" meaning "in a horizontal position".

ACCESSIBILITY

Considered a beginner-friendly hike due to its wide, well-maintained trails.
