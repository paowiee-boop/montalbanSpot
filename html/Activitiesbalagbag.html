ACTIVITIES YOU CAN DO IN MT. BALAGBAG

The primary activity on Mt. Balagbag is hiking due to its accessible trail, making it a popular choice for beginners; however, you can also engage in activities like mountain biking, running, 4x4 driving, nature photography, camping, and sightseeing on the mountain, which is particularly known for its views of Metro Manila from the summit.

ACTIVITIES

  • Hiking: The most common activity, with a relatively easy trail suitable for most fitness levels.
  • Trail running: The wide trail is also used by runners for training.
  • Mountain biking: Some bikers use the trails for riding.
  • 4x4 driving: The access road to the jump-off point is suitable for off-road vehicles.
  • Nature photography: The diverse flora and fauna along the trail provide opportunities for photography.
  • Camping: Overnight stays are possible for those looking for a longer experience.
  • Sightseeing: Enjoy panoramic views of the surrounding area, including Metro Manila, from the summit.
